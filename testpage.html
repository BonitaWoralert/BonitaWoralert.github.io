TP

Scream Jam 2023

Created in 7 days for Scream Jam 2023 with Isaac Dexter and Ayk Perera.

TP is a silly (and scary) horror game inspired by P.T. where you search for the toilet in an ever-changing house.
Managing poop is vital, as running around too much or facing too many scary sights will leave your pants positively soiled.
